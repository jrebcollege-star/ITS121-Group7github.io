Client FAQ

Everything you need to know about partnering with a fully online BPO.

How does a fully online BPO ensure reliability?

+

Our decentralized model is our greatest strength. Unlike physical offices that are vulnerable to local power outages or transit strikes, our workforce is distributed. We require all agents to have secondary power (UPS) and backup internet connections (fiber + 5G) to maintain 99.9% uptime.

What is the typical time-to-hire?

+

Depending on the technical requirements, we can typically deploy a vetted team within 2 to 4 weeks. For high-stakes roles like IPv4 management, we ensure specialized training is completed before the go-live date.

How do you handle sensitive client data?

+

We implement strict endpoint security protocols. This includes encrypted connections (VPN), multi-factor authentication (MFA), and strictly controlled access to client environments. We can also adapt to your specific internal security software requirements.

Can we scale the team size on short notice?

+

Yes. Our agile recruitment pipeline allows us to scale up during peak seasons or high-growth phases. We maintain a "ready-to-deploy" talent pool to ensure minimal friction when adding seats.

Do you provide technical advising or just staffing?

+

We offer specialized advising in niche sectors like IPv4 brokerage and iGaming operations. We don't just provide "warm bodies"; we provide subject matter experts who can help optimize your workflows and documentation processes.
